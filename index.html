Why this works?

During a read of a paragraph, along with the reading of words, our mind tends to read spaces between them as well so costing us 250 words per minute, but by eliminating the spaces between them your mind only just by focusing on words, you can speed read up to 500 words per minute. But why this method is not used for long text reads?

Spaces between words are just for sake of pause in your reading and let the reader take a moment in your life to understand the deep meaning of the text and connect the dots. Slow reading isnâ€™t bad as long as you can take out the meaning of the text :)
